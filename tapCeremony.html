TAP Ceremony

Welcome to the ceremony page. Here, we unveil the various ways you can engage with our exhibition.

Starting Gesture:

To begin the opening gesture, position both hands side by side while keeping them open. Then, execute a swift circular motion. This action will initiate the awaited tea ceremony.

Drop Gesture:

To perform the Drop Gesture, start by making a fist just above the tea bowl. Then, release your fist as if you're gently dropping something into the bowl. As if by magic, your projected flower will burst into life, unfolding its petals before your eyes, mirroring the beauty of dropping tea leaves into the boiled water. Watch in delight as the act of dropping invokes a symphony of growth and colour.

Swipe Gesture:

To perform the Swipe Gesture, execute a swift and fluid swipe motion across the bowl using your hand. This motion seamlessly whisks you to the next flower of the selected country. This swipe interaction enables you to experience each different flower of the selected culture before the exhibition moves onto the next country.

Pinch Gesture:

To perform the pinch gesture, position your left hand horizontally over the bowl and bring your index finger and thumb close together. Observe the projected flower growing larger as you do this.

Tap Gesture:

To perform the Tap Gesture, gently place one finger outside the tea bowl and tap on the water's surface. Watch in awe as your projected flower gracefully withers away, its petals drifting off with the breeze. In its place, a fresh bloom will emerge, painting the moment anew with its vibrant colours.
